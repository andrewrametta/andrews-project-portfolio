Hi, I am Andrew Rametta. I am a new software developer living in Tampa, FL. I spent the first part of my career in the nonprofit world and my main motivator is still helping make people’s lives better.

I am looking for a great team to join. I bring a unique perspective through my experiences in the nonprofit world. My favorite thing about software development is knowing I will never know everything. I am a life long learner and it excites me to know that learning is a part of the job.

A little about me...

I became a software developer because I wanted a career that required me to use my brain everyday. I enjoy solving problems and difficult challenges. I enjoy working with others and truly believe that 2 heads are better then 1. (Well, most of the time) I think having a diverse group of people with vastly different perspectives makes for the most creative teams with the greatest results.

When I’m not coding I am usually sweating in a small hot dark room... doing yoga. I can’t go a day without reading Goodnight Moon to my 16 month son atleast 5 times in a row. The kitchen is my favorite room in the house. I like to open up my fridge and pretending I am competing on an episode of Chopped.
